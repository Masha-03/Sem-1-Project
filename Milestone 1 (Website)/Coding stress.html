Suggestions on how 'Computer and Network Security'should evolve

AI and Machine Learning:
+ AI can analyze large amounts of data quickly and identify unusual patterns or actions that could mean a security breach
+ Leading to faster detection
+ Adapt and learn from the attacks to patch and cover known vulnerabilities

Zero Trust Architecture:(?)
+ Does not trust any entity, wheter outside or inside network
+ Reduces the risk of attacks and suspicious activities
+ Access is obtained only through strict identification and contextual factors
+ Model is hard to manage and easily confusing, planning is required to use the model to its full potential

Quantum Computing:
+ Poses risk to information security
+ Organizations need to consider these attacks and invest in new cryptographic securities to protect themselves in the long-term

Cybersecurity for IOT:
+ Expanding surface of connected devices bring growing weak points
+ Weak security measures
+ Combat weak points by isolating IOT devices from important systems

Regulations and Compliance:
+ Data regulations become more strict
+ Organizations need to invest and focus complying with these regulations
+ Pressure and penalties will be given should companies fail to abide by the standard

Human Factor and Social Engineering:
+ Conduct programmes to increase security awareness among employees
+ Invest in and use detection systems to find and prevent any social engineering attacks

Blockchain for Security:
+ Difficult to cover tamper-evident tracks
+ Used for safer identity management
